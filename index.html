EnglishBridge Bangalore

English learning built on presence, trust, and real conversation

We are creating an in-person teaching initiative for underserved children in Bangalore, with human connection at the center and AI used carefully for assignments, revision, and extra practice.

What We Are Building

A local education effort designed to feel warm, serious, and human.

Our first commitment is simple: show up in person. We want classrooms where children are encouraged to speak, read, ask questions, and grow more comfortable with English through repeated interaction with people who care.

Technology has a role, but not the starring role. We see AI as a quiet layer that can help with assignments, prompts, pronunciation support, vocabulary review, and practice beyond classroom hours.

In Person First

Conversation-led sessions

We want spoken English to become less intimidating through group activities, listening, reading aloud, and everyday dialogue.

AI as Support

Practice beyond class

Assignments can become more personal through vocabulary drills, reflection prompts, and adaptive follow-up built around each learner.
