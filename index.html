EnglishBridge Bangalore

English learning built on trust and real practice.

A classroom-first platform for underserved children in Bangalore. Teachers lead, students practise, and progress is tracked — all in one place.

📚
Lesson-based learning Admins create structured lessons with questions that students work through at their own pace.
📈
Progress you can see Every answer saved, every lesson tracked. Students and teachers know exactly where they stand.
🤝
Human connection first Technology supports the teacher — it never replaces the relationship at the heart of learning.

How it works

Admins build the content

Teachers and coordinators log in as admins to create classes, add lessons, and write the questions students will answer.

For students

Students learn and track

Students log in, pick their class, open a lesson, type their answers, and save progress. Everything is stored so they can resume anytime.

Our approach

Simple, consistent, human

No complicated setup. No algorithms making decisions. Just clear lessons, honest progress tracking, and a teacher who shows up.
